Gesture Guide

Left Blink

Single blink to Left Click.

Right Blink

Single blink to Right Click.

Drag & Drop

Hold Left Blink (1.5s) to start dragging. Blink left again to drop.

Auto-copies selection on drop.

Paste

Hold Right Blink (1.5s) to paste clipboard content.

Scrolling

Stare at Top/Bottom bars to scroll up/down.

App Switching

Stare at Left/Right bars to Alt-Tab.
