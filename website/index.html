Built for developers

  • 👁

    Eye tracking

    MediaPipe Face Mesh drives the cursor. Gaze at edges to scroll or switch apps.

  • 🎤

    Voice control

    Google Speech-to-Text or local Whisper. Dictate into any focused input.

  • ⌨

    Blink to click

    Left wink = click, right wink = right-click. Hold for drag; no hardware required.

  • 🖥

    Works everywhere

    IDE, browser, Overleaf, terminal. Scroll targets the pane you last clicked.

Quick start

  1. Download and install for your OS.
  2. Grant camera (and optionally microphone) access when prompted.
  3. Use the Control Panel to start/stop tracking, adjust sensitivity, and choose speech engine.
  4. Move your head to move the cursor; wink to click; gaze at top/bottom edges to scroll.

For setup details, API keys (e.g. Google Speech), and troubleshooting, see the Documentation.
